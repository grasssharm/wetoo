We cleaned the data by extracting relevant information related to sex-related offences and locations in Brooklyn. This involved filtering out irrelevant data, removing duplicates, and ensuring consistency. The cleaned data accurately represented the occurrences of such offences or crimes in Brooklyn, providing a reliable basis for analysis and mapping.

We utilized Mapbox along with custom HTML and JavaScript codes to geocode crime locations, land uses, and amenities. This allowed us to create interactive maps that present this information in a clear and understandable manner for the public.

Lastly, we built our website to organize all quantitative crime map data along with qualitative stories gathered. The website aims to provide all relevant information about sex-related offences or crimes in Brooklyn, presenting both quantitative data and qualitative narratives in an organized and accessible manner for users.
